About

Daniel Afonso

I'm final year student in Bachelor of Computer Engineering(BSc) at University of Minho.

Interests

One of my favourite fields of Computer Science is programming, and i carry a especially interest for web and mobile development. My base of knowledge are C and Java, and currently i'm starting with PHP.

Music

I consider music an indispensable gear for my work and health, so sometimes i will blog some of my favourite bands over here, so keep in touch!

Main focus

Currently I'm mostly working with Java and Html, developing a project to Distributed Systems about concurrency, and working on my personal web site.

In my free time, i'm learning Foundation, it is made by ZURB, a product design company in Campbell, California. I like simplicity and this framework gives me a pretty good basements to start to develop my web sites.
